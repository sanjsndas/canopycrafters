Privacy Policy

At CanopyCrafters, your privacy is of paramount importance to us. This Privacy Policy document outlines the types of information we collect from you, how it is used, stored, and safeguarded. We commit ourselves to ensuring transparency and compliance with relevant data protection regulations.

1. Information We Collect

We may collect and process the following data about you:

  • Personal Identification Data: including your name, address, email, phone number, and other contact details.
  • Usage Data: information about how you access and use our services, including browser type, IP address, pages visited, and interaction times.
  • Cookies and Tracking Technologies: we use cookies to enhance your experience, understand site usage and for targeted marketing.
  • Feedback and Communication: any messages, comments, or requests you submit to us through our contact forms or customer feedback channels.

We also collect demographic information such as your age range, gender, and interests to better tailor our services and marketing efforts to your preferences. We use advanced analytics tools to monitor trends and improve customer experience while ensuring data anonymity and aggregated reporting to protect individual identities.

Our data collection methods are vetted regularly to comply with the latest privacy legislations such as the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) and California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA), ensuring lawful, fair, and transparent processing of your personal data.

2. How We Use Your Information

The data we collect serves the following purposes:

  • To provide, operate and maintain our services and website effectively and efficiently.
  • To personalize your experience and respond to customer service requests swiftly.
  • For marketing purposes, including sending you promotional material if you consent to receive them.
  • To analyze website usage and improve our offerings leveraging aggregated and anonymized data.
  • To comply with legal and regulatory obligations.

Beyond these, we also use your data to conduct satisfaction surveys, solicit feedback on improvements, and notify you about important updates or changes to our services tailored to your interests and needs. We employ machine learning models responsibly to provide personalized recommendations for tree care based on your purchase history and interactions.

Our legal compliance extends to sharing your data with law enforcement or regulators when mandated by law or necessary to protect our rights, properties, or safety of users and the public. We always seek to minimize data sharing and only provide the minimum necessary information.

3. Data Storage and Security

We adopt stringent technical and organizational measures to protect your data from unauthorized access, loss, or misuse. These include:

  • Encryption of sensitive data during transmission.
  • Secure server environments with access control and regular security audits.
  • Secure destruction and deletion policies to ensure that data is disposed of safely when no longer needed.
  • Staff training on data privacy and security protocols.

Our infrastructure is hosted on ISO 27001 certified data centers with multi-layered firewalls, intrusion prevention systems, and continuous penetration testing conducted internally and externally. We use modern security frameworks such as TLS 1.3 for all internet communications and multi-factor authentication to secure access to our backend systems containing personal data.

Regular backups are maintained, encrypted, and stored securely to prevent data loss while ensuring GDPR-compliant retention and deletion policies. We promptly patch security vulnerabilities and maintain compliance certifications to uphold the highest standards in data protection.

4. Sharing Your Data

CanopyCrafters does not sell or share your personal data with third parties except:

  • With trusted partners and service providers who assist in our operations under confidentiality agreements.
  • As required by law, legal process, or to protect the rights, safety, or property of CanopyCrafters or others.
  • When acquiring or selling business assets where data transfer is part of the transaction.

We carefully vet all third parties for compliance with data protection laws and conduct regular audits to ensure adherence to our strict privacy commitments. You can request details of the partners with whom we share your data at any time. Data shared is limited and encrypted during transfer.

Additionally, anonymous aggregated data may be shared with industry analysts, research groups, or advertisers to provide insights into market trends, helping us continuously improve. Your personal details are never included in such data sets without explicit consent.

5. Your Data Protection Rights

Depending on your location and relevant laws, you may have the rights to:

  • Access the personal data we hold about you.
  • Request correction of inaccuracies in your personal data.
  • Request the deletion or restriction of processing of your data.
  • Object to the processing of your personal data under certain conditions.
  • Withdraw consent for marketing communications at any time.
  • Data portability requests where supported by law.

We maintain transparent and user-friendly processes to help you exercise these rights, including dedicated support and secure verification protocols. Requests will be handled promptly, typically within 30 days or as required by relevant legislation.

If you feel your privacy rights have been violated, you may also lodge a complaint with the appropriate supervisory authority in your jurisdiction. We participate fully in investigations and cooperate to resolve issues responsibly.

6. Cookies and Tracking

Our website uses cookies and similar tracking technologies to improve functionality and user experience. These include:

  • Essential Cookies: required for the basic operation of the site.
  • Performance Cookies: which collect anonymous data on site usage.
  • Functional Cookies: for remembering user preferences and settings.
  • Targeting or Advertising Cookies: used to deliver relevant advertisements.

You can manage cookie preferences in your browser settings. Disabling cookies may affect certain website features.

We offer cookie consent management frameworks allowing you to opt-out from non-essential cookies, and we provide detailed information about each cookie on our Cookie Policy page.

Tracking technologies complement our usage analytics, enabling heat-mapping, behavior analysis, session recordings (with consent), and marketing effectiveness measurement, always with strict data privacy controls and anonymization measures.

7. Children’s Privacy

CanopyCrafters does not knowingly collect or solicit data from anyone under 13 years of age. If you believe your child has provided us with personal data, please contact us immediately and we will delete the information.

We implement age verification and parental consent mechanisms where required, raising awareness on safe online practices and protecting minors from data exposure and marketing communications.

8. Data Retention

We retain your personal information only as long as necessary for the purposes outlined in this policy or as required by law.

Retention periods vary by data type and purpose. For example, transactional data is retained for up to 7 years for financial and legal compliance, while marketing contact data is retained until consent is withdrawn.

Data minimization guides our retention policies, storing only what's essential and securely deleting or anonymizing data when no longer needed.

9. Links to Other Websites

Our website may contain links to external websites not operated by us. We are not responsible for the content or privacy practices of these sites and recommend reviewing their policies before providing personal information.

We encourage users to exercise caution and review terms carefully, as these sites may collect or share your information differently, and we cannot guarantee their compliance with privacy regulations.

Notable changes will be communicated via email or website notifications to keep you informed of any material updates impacting your data rights or processing practices.
